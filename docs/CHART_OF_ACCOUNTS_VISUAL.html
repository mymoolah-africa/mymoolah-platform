6 Solvency Rules & Balance Equations

CRITICAL — Client Fund Safeguarding (SARB Requirement)
Client Float Liability (2100-01-01) ≤ Bank (1100-01-01) + Σ Supplier Floats (1200-10-XX)
Users' funds must be fully backed. If violated: HALT deposits/withdrawals.
CRITICAL — Wallet Aggregate Integrity
Σ (wallets.balance) = Net credit balance of 2100-01-01 Tolerance: R0.01
CRITICAL — Trial Balance (Zero Variance)
Σ (debit-normal balances) − Σ (credit-normal balances) = 0 Tolerance: R0.01
Journal Entry Balance
For every JournalEntry: Σ debit lines = Σ credit lines (enforced at write time)
Voucher Cover
Net credit balance of 2500-01-01 = Σ (active unredeemed voucher amounts)
No Negative Wallets
For all wallets: balance ≥ 0 (enforced by Wallet.debit() + SELECT FOR UPDATE)
