to open a Corporate Account with VALR.


1.  PURPOSE OF THE CORPORATE ACCOUNT

    MyMoolah is a registered Third-Party Payment Provider (TPPP) under
    the Payments Association of South Africa (PASA), sponsored by
    Standard Bank of South Africa. We operate a banking-grade digital
    wallet and payments platform serving consumers and small businesses
    in South Africa.

    The purpose of the VALR Corporate Account is to facilitate the
    ZAR-to-USDC conversion required for our cross-border remittance
    product, MoolahMove. MoolahMove enables MyMoolah wallet holders in
    South Africa to send money to family and friends in other African
    countries via regulated payment corridors.

    Specifically, the VALR account will be used to:

    •  Convert ZAR to USDC (Solana) as part of the MoolahMove
       cross-border remittance flow
    •  Withdraw USDC to our off-ramp partner (Yellow Card) for
       conversion to local fiat currency and disbursement to recipients
       in SADC countries
    •  Maintain a USDC float balance for operational liquidity in the
       remittance corridor

    The end user (MyMoolah wallet holder) does not interact with
    cryptocurrency at any point. The crypto conversion is an invisible
    infrastructure layer handled entirely by the platform.


2.  NATURE OF BUSINESS OPERATIONS

    MyMoolah Treasury Platform provides the following services:

    •  Digital wallet — secure value storage, peer-to-peer transfers,
       QR code payments
    •  Value-Added Services — airtime, mobile data, electricity, bill
       payments, and digital vouchers (via Flash, MobileMart, EasyPay)
    •  Real-time bank payments — PayShap integration via Standard Bank
       (Request to Pay and Rapid Payments)
    •  Cross-border remittance — MoolahMove (ZAR → USDC → local fiat
       via VALR and Yellow Card)
    •  KYC-verified customer onboarding aligned with FICA requirements

    The platform is built on Node.js, PostgreSQL, and React, hosted on
    Google Cloud Platform with banking-grade security (TLS 1.3,
    AES-256-GCM encryption, ISO 27001 readiness).

    Production URLs:
    •  API:     api-mm.mymoolah.africa
    •  Wallet:  wallet.mymoolah.africa


3.  SOURCE OF FUNDS

    Funds deposited into the VALR Corporate Account will originate
    exclusively from the MyMoolah TPPP Creditor Account held at
    Standard Bank of South Africa.

    These funds represent:

    •  Consumer wallet balances — ZAR deposited by MyMoolah wallet
       holders via EFT, PayShap, or retail cash-in
    •  Prefunded float — operational float maintained by MyMoolah for
       MoolahMove remittance liquidity

    All funds entering the MyMoolah ecosystem are subject to:

    •  KYC/FICA verification of the originating wallet holder
    •  AML/CFT transaction monitoring
    •  Suspicious transaction reporting to the FIC
    •  Automated reconciliation and double-entry ledger controls

    MyMoolah does not accept cash deposits directly. All inflows are
    through regulated banking channels (Standard Bank) or authorised
    retail partners (EasyPay, Flash).
