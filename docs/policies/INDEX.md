## Executive Statement

MyMoolah (Pty) Ltd operates a banking-grade Treasury Platform under the sponsorship of Standard Bank of South Africa. This Corporate Policy Framework establishes the governance, compliance, risk management, and operational standards required of a Mojaloop-compliant, PASA-regulated financial services provider.

These policies are designed to satisfy — and where possible exceed — the requirements of:

- **South African Regulatory Framework**: Financial Intelligence Centre Act (FICA), Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), National Payment System Act (NPS Act), Prevention and Combating of Corrupt Activities Act (PRECCA), Companies Act 71 of 2008, Electronic Communications and Transactions Act (ECT Act), Protected Disclosures Act 26 of 2000
- **International Standards**: FATF 40 Recommendations, ISO 27001 (Information Security), ISO 22301 (Business Continuity), ISO 31000 (Risk Management), ISO 37001 (Anti-Bribery), Basel Committee Principles, King IV Code on Corporate Governance
- **Industry Frameworks**: Mojaloop FSPIOP, PCI DSS Awareness, SOC 2 Type II Readiness, NIST Cybersecurity Framework

All employees, contractors, directors, and third-party service providers acting on behalf of MyMoolah are bound by these policies.
