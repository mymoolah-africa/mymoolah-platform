Executive Overview

The commercial opportunity is wallet utility at mainstream card-enabled merchants, without adding cash withdrawal.

Platform Positioning

MyMoolah is a South African banking-grade Treasury Platform and PASA TPPP-certified fintech platform, with Standard Bank of South Africa as its sponsor bank. The platform provides digital wallet, payment, disbursement, value-added services, KYC, ledger, reconciliation, and compliance capabilities.

The platform is built around the MMwallet, a secure stored-value wallet used by verified customers to receive, hold, and spend funds through permitted wallet use cases.

PASA TPPP-certified Standard Bank sponsor bank KYC/CDD controls Double-entry ledger
Proposed OneGate Scope

OneGate issues a pre-funded virtual Mastercard debit card linked to an eligible MMwallet. Once linked, the card can be tokenised into an NFC wallet such as Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Garmin Pay.

The wallet holder can then use available MMwallet balance for card-present merchant point-of-sale transactions in South Africa.

Audit And Settlement Controls

The programme should be implemented as an auditable wallet-to-card spend rail, not as a cash access rail.

No Negative Wallets Authorisation is approved only where sufficient wallet value is available.
Double-Entry Ledger Every posted financial event must balance debits and credits to the cent.
Idempotent References Duplicate authorisation, advice, or reversal messages must not create duplicate wallet debits.
Partner Float OneGate prefunding is tracked as a partner-float position and reconciled to settlement evidence.
Reversal Handling Declines, reversals, expiries, chargebacks, and adjustments require auditable correction paths.
Monitoring Evidence Authorisation, wallet debit, settlement, and reconciliation events remain traceable.

Phase-One Boundaries

These boundaries keep the first release commercially useful while remaining regulatory-safe.

In Scope

  • South African wallet holders.
  • South African card-present merchant POS spend.
  • OneGate-issued pre-funded virtual Mastercard debit cards.
  • NFC tokenisation where supported by device wallet providers.
  • Real-time MyMoolah authorisation against available MMwallet balance.
  • Partner-float and settlement reconciliation.

Out Of Scope

  • ATM cash withdrawal.
  • Retail cash-back at till.
  • Cash-out or cash-equivalent withdrawal mechanisms.
  • Cross-border card use in phase one.
  • Unapproved gambling, quasi-cash, or high-risk expansion.
  • Any use case not separately approved by commercial, legal, compliance, and technical teams.
This document is intended for partner evaluation and solution alignment. Final implementation remains subject to commercial agreement, legal review, scheme requirements, sponsor-bank requirements, integration design, and mutually agreed operational procedures.

Partnership Outcome

The integration extends wallet utility without changing the cash-withdrawal boundary.

For OneGate, the integration creates a scalable wallet-linked card programme with a defined funding source, clear settlement support, and a controlled South African phase-one scope.

For MyMoolah, it extends the MMwallet from in-app payments and disbursement use cases into everyday NFC merchant payments, while keeping wallet balance, ledger integrity, audit evidence, and regulatory controls inside the MyMoolah Treasury Platform.

Commercial terms, including transaction revenue share, are to be agreed separately with OneGate.
